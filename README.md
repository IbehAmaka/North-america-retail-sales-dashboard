# North America Retail Supply Chain & Sales Analysis
**Excel · Pivot Tables · Business Intelligence**

## Overview
Sales performance analysis for a North American retail business (2014–2017),  
covering regional trends, discount impact, and profit drivers across 9,995 transactions.

**Tool:** Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Conditional Formatting)  
**Dataset:** 9,995 rows · 23 columns (Sales, Profit, Discount, Returns, Ship Mode)

## Key Findings

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Sales | $2.3M |
| Total Profit | $286K |
| Total Orders | 9,994 |
| Overall Profit Margin | 12.47% |
| Total Returns | 800 |

### Executive
- 2017 is the highest sales year; West region is the top revenue contributor

### Regional Performance
- West region leads overall; Central region is the only region with negative growth
- Technology category dominates across all regions

### Discount Analysis
- Discounts above 30% consistently generate negative profit (avg loss: **$719.43 per transaction**)
- The 20% discount bracket has the highest return rate — suggesting product quality issues
- Negative relationship between discount depth and sales volume

## Business Recommendations
1. **Eliminate discounts above 40%** — consistently unprofitable across all categories
2. **Investigate Central region stagnation** — only region with negative growth trajectory
3. **Review 20% discount products** — high return rate signals quality or expectation mismatch
